## Your portal account

The customer portal is where you submit and track support requests with your provider. Your account is scoped to your organization: you see only the tickets your organization has access to.

### Signing up

If you have never used the portal before, your first email to support will create an account automatically. You will receive a verification message; click the link inside 60 minutes to set a password and finish setup.

### Signing in

Sign in with your email and password at `/`. If you forgot your password, click **Reset password** on the sign-in page; you will receive an email with a one-time link.

### Enabling MFA (optional)

You can optionally protect your portal account with a TOTP authenticator app. Open **My account** -> **Security** and click **Enable MFA**. Scan the QR code with the authenticator app of your choice and confirm the six-digit code to bind it. From then on every sign-in prompts for a code.

If you lose your device, sign in with email and password, then use one of the recovery codes the system gave you at enrollment. If you also lost the recovery codes, contact your provider's support team to reset the second factor.
