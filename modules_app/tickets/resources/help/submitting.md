## Submitting a ticket

You can submit a support ticket in any of three ways:

1. **The customer portal**: sign in at `/` and click **New ticket**. The portal pre-fills your contact details and lets you attach files. You can track every ticket you have submitted from the same page.
2. **Email**: send a message to the address your provider has published. The system threads replies on the same ticket automatically as long as the subject preserves the ticket number.
3. **The embedded widget**: if your provider has embedded the widget on a site you visit, click the help icon to start a new ticket without leaving the page.

### What happens next

Once submitted you will receive an acknowledgement with the ticket number. The agent assigned to your ticket will reply through the same channel; replies land both in the portal and in your email so you can answer from whichever you prefer.

If you do not have a portal account yet, your first email submission will create one and ask you to verify the address.
