## Working the ticket queue

The ticket queue is your view of every open ticket you have access to. Filter, sort, and bulk-act through the controls above the list.

### Picking up a ticket

A ticket can be assigned to you in three ways:

- An automation rule routed it to your team or to you specifically.
- Another agent reassigned it during a hand-off.
- You claimed it yourself from the queue.

To claim an unassigned ticket, open it and click **Assign to me** in the right column. Once claimed, the requester sees you as the named agent on their portal copy.

### Replying

Use the reply composer at the bottom of the ticket. **Reply** sends to the requester; **Internal note** stays agent-only and never reaches any client surface, even an Organization Admin. Pick canned responses, attach files, or insert a KB article link from the composer's toolbar.

### Status, priority, and tags

The right column carries the workflow controls. Transition status to **In progress** when you start work, to **Waiting on customer** when you have asked them a question, and to **Resolved** when the work is done. Re-open is one click if the requester writes back after resolution.

### Bulk actions

Select multiple tickets via the checkboxes and use the **Bulk** dropdown to assign, change status, add tags, or merge in one operation. The Audit log records who did what to every ticket touched.
