## Managing contacts

A **contact** is a person at one of the client organizations you support. Contacts can submit tickets, receive notifications, and (with the right role) administer their own organization's portal.

### Looking up a contact

Open **Contacts** from the agent navigation. The search box matches name, email, and phone number across every organization you have access to. The list is tenant-scoped: an agent only sees contacts in the organizations their role allows.

### Creating a contact

If a ticket arrives from an email address that is not yet known, the system creates an **accountless ticket** so the work can begin immediately. To promote that ticket's sender to a real contact, open the ticket and click **Create contact from sender**. The form pre-fills the email address; choose the organization the contact belongs to and save. The ticket is reassigned to that organization automatically.

### Merging duplicates

When two contacts represent the same person, open the older record, click **Merge into another contact**, and pick the target. All tickets, messages, attachments, and notification preferences move to the surviving record. The merged record is audit-logged and retrievable in compliance reports.
