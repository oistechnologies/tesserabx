## Finding knowledge-base articles

The knowledge base is a searchable collection of articles your support team has written for the most common questions. Articles can answer a question outright (so you do not need to wait for a reply) or supplement a ticket.

### Browsing

Open the **Knowledge base** link from the portal nav. Articles are grouped into categories; pick a category to drill in. The most-viewed articles in each category surface first.

### Searching

The search box at the top of the knowledge base matches title, body, and tags. Results are scoped to articles that are visible to you: public articles, articles your organization has been granted, and any pinned starters.

### Article visibility

Articles ship in three tiers:

- **Public** — anyone can read, even without signing in.
- **Organization-scoped** — readable only when you are signed in and your organization has been granted access. Useful for documentation about a feature your provider has enabled for some customers but not others.
- **Internal** — agent-only documentation. Never reaches any client surface, including organization administrators.
