# Welcome to TesseraBX

TesseraBX is a multi-tenant help-desk and customer support platform. This help system is the in-app reference for the people who use TesseraBX day to day, and for the developers who extend it with add-ons.

## What you can find here

- **Getting started**: short intros to the parts of TesseraBX you will use most often.
- **Module-specific help**: each major area (tickets, contacts, knowledge base, automation, SLAs, etc.) has its own section with task-focused pages.
- **Module Development** (developer-only): the live extension contract that ships with the product, mirrored from the project's `docs/EXTENSIONS.md`. Visible only to agents with the `help.developer` permission.

## Searching

The search box at the top of every help page searches across every page you are allowed to read. When AI is enabled the search is semantic (the box understands related concepts, not just literal text); when AI is off the same box still works using straightforward text matching. The behavior is identical from your point of view either way.

## Asking for help

Every section's pages are written and maintained by the team that owns that area of the product. If a page is unclear or missing, ask in the project's issue tracker and the owning module will pick it up.
